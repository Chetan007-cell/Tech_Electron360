THE NEW LENOVO GAMING LAPTOP IS A MONSTER!

by chetan vegad | 16/10/20

LENOVO LEGION 5I

Introduction:

Lenovo just launched it's new gaming laptop for hardcore gamers. Legion have been gaining attention of a lot of pc gamers,since the date of release. Because of it's Better Quality,Design and Performance. Back in 2017, Lenovo introduced the Legion family and I loved their Legion Y720. The leading brand has done this to distinguish its gaming system from the main IdeaPad line. Prior to the launch of Legion, Lenovo’s gaming machines stayed under the IdeaPad line labeled as “IdeaPadY” models. Legion has now emerged as all gaming and includes both laptops and pre-built desktops.

If the laptop model has both AMD and Intel options, then the laptop may end with an “i” to indicate an Intel CPU whereas if there is nothing indicated it has an AMD CPU. For example, the new Legion 5 comes with an AMD processor whereas the Legion 5i has an Intel Processor.To improve performance and get the most out of the legion series of laptops the company has introduced the next-generation thermal efficiencies of the Lenovo Legion Coldfront 2.0. The new cooling solution offers better airflow, lower system temperatures to keep the machine cool, quiet, and slim. There is a new feature called Dual Burn support, which pushes the CPU and GPU to the limit without the need for thermal throttling.

The medium-range Legion 5/5i will become available in 15.6 inch and 17.3-inch models whereas the Legion 7i is available in 15.6-inch size. It is important to note that Legion 7/7i is expected to be integrating the Q Control 3.0 software to overclock and under volt. Undervolt can significantly enhance CPU performance on some laptops. However, users will have to remain watchful about the system’s stability. Legion 7i is already available in some countries since May and will launch in other countries (like India) soon. The Legion 7i starts with the base Intel Core i5-10300H Processor with 4 Cores and 8 threads, along with 8 GB of ram and Nvidia GTX 1660Ti (6GB VRAM). The top model comes with the 8 Core Intel Core i7-10875H and the RTX 2080 MaxQ model along with a better display and 16 gigs of ram.
